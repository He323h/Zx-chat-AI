Mujhe apne poore app ka UI redesign karna hai "Claymorphism" design style me — 
jisme cards aur elements soft, puffy, 3D-clay jaisa dikhte hain (jaise dough/clay 
se bane ho), saath me professional aur consistent blue theme. Neeche diya gaya 
design system strictly follow karo har screen, har component pe.

=== DESIGN STYLE: CLAYMORPHISM ===
Har card/button/icon container me ye "clay effect" hona chahiye:
- Double shadow effect: ek HALKA shadow upar-left (light source jaisa) aur ek 
  DARKER shadow neeche-right (depth ke liye)
  Example CSS: box-shadow: -6px -6px 12px rgba(255,255,255,0.7), 
                            6px 6px 16px rgba(28,176,246,0.25);
- Border-radius BAHUT zyada rounded (24-32px) — sharp corners bilkul nahi
- Background thoda gradient ya solid pastel color (flat flat nahi, halka depth wala)
- Elements "uthe hue" (raised/embossed) lagne chahiye, jaise screen se bahar 
  ubhar rahe ho — flat design nahi

=== COLOR PALETTE (Blue-based Claymorphism) ===
- Primary Blue (clay base): #1CB0F6
- Primary Light Blue (highlight/shine): #6FD3FF
- Primary Dark Blue (shadow/depth): #0E8FD4
- Background: #EAF4FF (very light soft blue-white — clay base background)
- Card Background: #FFFFFF ya halka gradient (#FFFFFF → #F0F8FF)
- Success Green (clay style): #58CC02 with same double-shadow technique
- Accent Orange (streak/highlights): #FF9600
- Text Primary: #1A2B3C
- Text Secondary: #6B7785

RULE: Har header blue gradient (#1CB0F6 → #0E8FD4) me hoga, claymorphism shadow 
effect ke saath niche curve karta hua. Koi alag color ka full header kahin nahi.

=== CLAY CARD STYLE (sabse important) ===
- Border-radius: 28px (bahut rounded, soft pillow jaisa)
- Padding: 20px
- Background: white ya light pastel
- Shadow: dono taraf se — light shadow upar-left (white/light blue), 
  dark shadow neeche-right (blue tinted, halka blur zyada)
- Hover/press state: shadow thoda andar dabne jaisa (inset shadow) — 
  jaise clay ko dabaya ja raha ho

=== ICONS & BUTTONS ===
- Icon containers: round ya squircle, same clay shadow effect, 3D-pop jaisa lage
- Buttons: bulgy/raised look, jab press karo to thoda "dab" jaye (inset shadow), 
  bilkul flat button nahi — har button clay ka bana hua feel de
- Icons khud 3D-illustration style ke ho sake to best hai (jaise screenshot me 
  rocket/calendar icons the) — flat line icons ke bajaye thoda dimensional/cute style

=== SPACING SYSTEM ===
- Screen padding: 16-20px
- Gap between cards: 16px (clay cards ko thoda zyada breathing space chahiye 
  unki puffy nature ki wajah se)
- Gap between sections: 28px

=== TYPOGRAPHY ===
- Page Title: 22px Bold
- Section Heading: 18px Semi-Bold  
- Card Title: 16px Semi-Bold
- Body Text: 14px Regular
- Font: Poppins ya Inter — rounded, friendly font (claymorphism ke saath match karta hai)

=== LAYOUT RULES ===
1. Consistent bottom navigation honi chahiye, usme bhi clay-style icons (raised, 
   active tab thoda zyada "popped out" dikhna chahiye)
2. Progress bars bhi clay style me — track thoda dabela (inset), filled portion 
   raised/glossy
3. Empty states me cute 3D illustration daalo (jaise rocket/character), plain 
   text/icon nahi
4. Ads ko bhi clay card jaisa hi style do — round corners, soft shadow, 
   app ka hi part lage

Reference style: Soft UI / Claymorphism jaisa Dribbble pe trending hai — 
puffy 3D cards with dual soft shadows, bright blue color scheme, friendly 
rounded everything.

Ab is design system ke according mera poora app redesign karo — saari screens 
(Home, Streak Badges, Tense Learning Path, Word Arrange, Chat) consistent 
claymorphism blue theme me banao.
